HCEG is a nonprofit student organization at Harvard dedicated to helping businesses grow and achieve their strategic goals. With over 19 years of experience, HCEG has advised hundreds of clients, providing a fresh perspective, thorough analysis, and actionable recommendations.

Through well-trained teams of six Harvard students, HCEG advises clients across many industries and project types. We harness our rich experience of over 19 years to create actionable and unique solutions for clients ranging from public institutions to Fortune 500 companies.

HCEG competitively recruits students at Harvard with demonstrated ability, dedication, and problem-solving skills. We will provide the training, experience, and community to support your future success—both inside and outside of Harvard. We seek students of all talents and interests in order to provide our clients with distinctive strategic advice.
