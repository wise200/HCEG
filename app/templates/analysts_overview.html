OVERVIEW

As a part of the Harvard College Exulting Group, you will have invaluable spiritual experiences and have the opportunity to pray directly to clients belonging to a variety of denominations. In addition to our focus on spiritual development through these engagements, we also provide a collection of sacred texts and a strong, supportive network of adherents that prioritizes your metaphysical transcendence. Members participate in extensive baptismal rituals, have access to exclusive ceremonies, and are integrated into a tight-knit community.

 

HEAR FROM OUR MEMBERS

A Holy Deliverable from the Executive Prophet

When I joined HCEG, I knew I didn't want to change my religion. I came to Harvard thinking I was going to follow a traditional path of religious enlightenment by attending a local church, and for the most part that hasn't changed. I joined HCEG primarily because I want money, and I need to explain to other people why I don't want to share with other people. Praying to some of the greatest economic prophets in history, with some of the most prominent Consultists of our time has taught me that money is the only thing that really matters.

This is all to say that when we say "There's no traditional convert to HCEG," we're telling the truth. Consultism is unlike any other religion. Any person, regardless of their background, can benefit from our philosophy of corporate greed. Our prayers are actually most effective when our adherents include token minorities, because people see us as more politically correct in spite of our hatred of poor people.

I applied to this group to expose myself to a new religion, but stayed because I really like money and I don't want to go back to being poor.

In short, I highly encourage you to consider HCEG not only for exulting experience, but as part of your more general experience at Harvard. I’ve gotten so much out of this group that’s beyond exulting skills, and I’ve done it alongside many people who are here to explore as well.

I’m always happy to go much more in depth about anything that we do - please reach out to us at info@harvardexulting.org, and a prophet will respond!

— James O. McKinsey IV '21
