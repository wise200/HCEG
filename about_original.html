CLIENTELE

The Harvard College Consulting Group partners with 36 large businesses, start-ups, non-profits, and public institutions each year. Our group has developed experience and expertise across many industries and project types over its rich 19-year history. Since 2016, HCCG has advised clients with a combined valuation of over $5.6 trillion. HCCG has a team of well-trained Harvard students with the skills to complete a project for you, whatever that may entail.

SERVICES

HCCG serves as an advisor to management teams of large businesses, start-ups, non-profits, and public institutions. Using our teams' quantitative and qualitative skills, HCCG addresses complex problems to help clients capitalize on opportunities. For 19 years, our group has offered cost-effective solutions and helped clients with a variety of project types, including product development, entry and expansion, sales and marketing, data and modeling, and process improvement.
